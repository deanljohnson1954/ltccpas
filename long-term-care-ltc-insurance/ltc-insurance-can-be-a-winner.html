LTC Insurance Can Be a WIN-WIN

LTC INSURANCE CAN BE A WIN-WIN

There is no need to self-insure when you have the ideal insurance policy that pays up to $12,000 a month. Here's how the numbers work for a couple aged 50:

The Numbers: A 50-Year-Old Couple

ScenarioDetails
Single premium (today's cost)$234,048
One year of LTC in skilled nursing facility$288,000 (less than the premium!)
Immediate cash surrender value$158,288 (if cancelled day after purchase)

What Happens in Each Scenario

Scenario 1: LTC is Needed After an Accident

If the couple is in a car crash today and needs long term care for life, the insurer will pay up to $24,000 a month. That's $10,080,000 assuming a combined life span of 35 years — for both insureds.

Scenario 2: LTC is Never Needed

If LTC is never needed, the policy will pay a death benefit on the second insured to die of $400,000 — a guaranteed gain of $165,952 over the original premium.

Scenario 3: Cancel at Age 85

If the insureds want to cancel the policy at age 85, they will receive the cash surrender value of $301,992 — a guaranteed gain of $67,944.
