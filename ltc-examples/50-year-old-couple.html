LTC Illustration: 50-Year-Old Couple

Comparing Single Pay, 10-Pay, and Pay-to-95 on a hybrid LTC policy — same benefits, your choice of cash flow.

Figures based on a policy illustration for insureds aged 50, Preferred Non-Tobacco, California. Individual results will vary based on age, health, and policy design.

What Bay Area LTC Care Actually Costs

Before examining the policy, consider what the alternative costs. A semi-private room in a Bay Area Skilled Nursing Facility currently runs $15,000 per month — $180,000 per year per person. For two insureds, joint care at that rate costs $360,000 per year. Self-insuring this risk requires maintaining and protecting a substantial reserve — indefinitely.

The Policy

A single-premium hybrid LTC policy is not an expense — it is a financial instrument that wins in every scenario. It is ideally suited for those with idle monies sitting in CDs, money market accounts, or uninvested cash earning modest returns. A one-time premium repositions those assets into a policy that provides guaranteed, tax-free LTC benefits, a guaranteed death benefit, and immediate cash surrender value from day one.

For a C Corporation owner, the LTC premium portion of $121,676 may be fully tax-deductible. At a 30% effective rate, this produces an estimated tax saving of $36,500, dramatically reducing the effective capital at risk:

All three options provide identical coverage. Single Pay is ideal for repositioning an idle lump sum in a single move. 10-Pay and Pay to 95 suit those who prefer to preserve liquidity and spread payments over time — while still locking in full coverage from day one.

The Bottom Line

“LTC insurance is always a win-win for those with idle monies to reposition.”
— Withbert W. Payne, CPA, CGMA, FCA

Every dollar deposited as a single premium is either: (1) returned to heirs as a guaranteed, income-tax-free death benefit exceeding the premium — a gain of $156,100 in Scenario A — or (2) leveraged to pay unlimited, tax-free LTC benefits, eliminating any need to self-insure. Premiums are guaranteed never to increase. The policy also provides an immediate, accessible cash surrender value from day one. For those with idle monies in low-yield vehicles, this is a straightforward repositioning — not an expense.
