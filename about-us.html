Withbert W. Payne, CPA

Certified Public Accountant · Licensed Insurance Agent

Withbert W. Payne is a Certified Public Accountant and licensed insurance agent with over 25 years of experience in financial planning and insurance optimization. His unique dual expertise as both a CPA and licensed insurance agent gives clients a distinct advantage — the ability to evaluate LTC policies not just for coverage adequacy, but for financial efficiency and tax implications.

Withbert began offering insurance review services after recognizing that most families were either underinsured, overpaying, or both. His mission: bring the analytical rigor of a CPA to the world of long-term care insurance. He personally reviews every client's policy, explains the findings in plain language, and recommends solutions tailored to their specific financial situation — all at no cost to the client.

He has helped hundreds of families across the country navigate the complex landscape of long-term care insurance — from first-time buyers to clients seeking to optimize existing policies. His CPA background means he always views LTC planning in the context of your complete financial picture, including tax advantages, estate planning considerations, and cash flow impact.

Why Work With a CPA?

Most insurance agents are licensed to sell — but few have the financial training to audit. As CPAs, we evaluate every policy through the lens of tax efficiency, cash flow impact, and long-term financial security. That's a level of analysis you won't find at a typical insurance agency. We don't just ask "what does this policy cover?" — we ask "is this the most financially efficient way to protect your assets?"

Our Approach

We believe that the best LTC coverage is coverage that fits your specific situation — your health, your assets, your family structure, and your financial goals. Our process is built around listening first, then delivering a comprehensive, unbiased analysis.

  • We listen first — your needs drive our recommendations
  • We audit your existing policy before suggesting changes
  • We compare carriers across the full market
  • We explain everything in plain language — no jargon
  • We never charge fees
  • We recommend policies with lifetime benefit options

Our Credentials

Withbert W. Payne holds active CPA licensure and insurance licenses in multiple states, allowing us to serve clients nationwide. Our dual credential set — CPA plus licensed insurance agent — is rare in the LTC insurance industry and gives our clients a significant analytical advantage when evaluating their coverage options.

Why We Do This

Long-term care is one of the most significant financial risks facing Americans today. The statistics are stark: 70% of people over 65 will need some form of long-term care, and the average lifetime cost exceeds $1.8 million. Yet most people remain dramatically underinsured — or have no coverage at all. We founded LTCCPAs.com to change that, one family at a time.
