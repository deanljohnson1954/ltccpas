Long Term Care Insurance with A Guaranteed Death Benefit

Long Term Care Insurance with A Guaranteed Death Benefit

Did you know that there is more than one option when it comes to long term care insurance?

Traditional Long Term Care Insurance

With traditional long term care insurance, you pay your premium, and if you need long-term care due to age or illness, the policy pays out a daily or monthly benefit. Some people feel that if they die without ever needing long-term care, they have "wasted" the premiums paid.

Long Term Care Insurance That Pays a Death Benefit

We specialize in LTC policies that pay lifetime benefits and a death benefit if there are no LTC claims. This means:

  • If you need long-term care, the policy pays out comprehensive benefits — for life
  • If you never need long-term care, your beneficiary receives a death benefit that, in most cases, will exceed the premiums you paid
  • The policy builds cash value that can be borrowed against at a net interest rate of 4%

It's a true win-win. You're covered if you need care, and your estate benefits if you don't. Contact us to learn more about an LTC insurance policy with a guaranteed death benefit.
