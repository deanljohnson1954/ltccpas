Cost/Benefits of a Single Premium Policy for an Affluent Client

THE AFFLUENT CLIENT

Why carry the risk of self-insuring when the cost of one year of LTC coverage can have lifetime benefits and, if the LTC policy is never utilized, the insurer pays more than the premiums paid?

Single Pay Premium Example for a 50-Year-Old Couple

The cost of a semi-private room in a skilled nursing facility is $12,000 a month in the San Francisco Bay area.

If LTC is Not Needed

Total cost of LTC for joint insureds (one year)$288,000
Single one-time LTC premium for a 50-year-old couple$234,408
Death benefit paid if LTC is never needed$400,000
Guaranteed gain to beneficiary$165,592

If LTC is Needed

Single one-time premium for a 50-year-old couple$234,048
Starting cash surrender value$158,288
Net premium cost for both joint insureds$75,760
If joint insureds need care for ten years, insurer pays$2,400,000

Policyholders may borrow against the cash surrender value at 8% annually.

"In short, LTC insurance is always a WIN-WIN for the affluent client!"
