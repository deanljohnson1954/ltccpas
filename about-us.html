About Us — Meet Withbert W. Payne, CPA

Chartered Accountant, CPA, and independent insurance broker with decades of experience in accounting and 25 years in LTC insurance

Withbert W. Payne, CPA, CA, CGMA

Withbert W. Payne is a Chartered Accountant (CA), Certified Public Accountant (CPA), and CGMA with decades of experience in accounting and finance — and 25 years as a licensed, independent insurance broker. This rare combination of credentials means every client receives the rigorous financial analysis of a seasoned CPA alongside the market expertise of a dedicated insurance professional.

Bert has been a licensed insurance broker since 2001, specializing exclusively in long-term care insurance. He personally reviews every client's policy, explains his findings in plain language, and recommends solutions tailored to each client's specific financial situation — all at no cost. His CPA background means he always views LTC planning in the context of the complete financial picture: tax advantages, estate planning considerations, and cash flow impact.

As an independent broker, Bert represents no single carrier. He compares policies across the full market to find the best fit for each client's health profile, assets, and goals. His only obligation is to you.

Why Work With a CPA?

Most insurance agents are licensed to sell — but few have the financial training to audit. As a CPA and Chartered Accountant, Bert evaluates every policy through the lens of tax efficiency, cash flow impact, and long-term financial security. That's a level of analysis you won't find at a typical insurance agency. He doesn't just ask "what does this policy cover?" — he asks "is this the most financially efficient way to protect your assets?"

Credentials & Education

Withbert W. Payne holds a credential set that is exceptionally rare in the LTC insurance industry — decades of experience as a CPA and Chartered Accountant, combined with 25 years as a licensed independent insurance broker.

  • CPA
    Certified Public Accountant — California
    Issued by the California Board of Accountancy. Requires rigorous examination, experience requirements, and continuing professional education.
  • FCA
    Fellow Chartered Accountant — Institute of Chartered Accountants in England & Wales
    The Fellow grade — the highest membership level — of the ICAEW, founded in 1880. Widely regarded as one of the most rigorous professional accounting qualifications in the world. Equivalent to the CPA in the United States. Earned through formal articleship training and examination at H. Foulkes Lynch School of Accountancy, London (1963–1968).
  • CGMA
    Chartered Global Management Accountant
    A joint designation of the AICPA and CIMA, recognizing expertise in management accounting and strategic financial leadership across global organizations.
  • GCE
    General Certificate of Education — Mathematics
    Oak Grove Boys’ School, Himalayas (1956–1961). Examination administered by the University of Cambridge.
  • CA
    Chartered Accountant Training — England & Wales
    H. Foulkes Lynch School of Accountancy, London (1963–1968). Professional accountancy training leading to Fellowship of the ICAEW.
