Complimentary Life & Long-Term Care Insurance Review

Most people review their investment portfolio regularly, but rarely review their life or long-term care protection. As a CPA, Chartered Accountant, and former internal auditor at an insurance company, Bert provides an independent review. Takes about 3 minutes.

How to Request Your Review

  1. Download the intake form below.
  2. Open the PDF in Adobe Acrobat Reader or any PDF viewer and type directly into the fields.
  3. Attach policy documents if you have them handy — no need to retype the details.
  4. Sign electronically — type your full legal name in the gold-bordered signature field.
  5. Email the completed form to bert.ltccpas@gmail.com.
  6. Bert will respond personally within one business day.

Why an Independent Review Matters

Bert is a CPA, Chartered Accountant, and former internal auditor at an insurance company — not a salesperson.
