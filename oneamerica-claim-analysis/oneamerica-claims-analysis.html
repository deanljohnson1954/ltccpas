OneAmerica Claims Analysis

OneAmerica Claims Analysis

OneAmerica is a prominent provider of long-term care insurance, and their claims data illustrates the real-world scope of LTC needs. The analysis of their largest claims shows how long, costly, and unpredictable long-term care can be.

What the Data Shows

The largest LTC claims paid by OneAmerica demonstrate that:

  • LTC needs can extend for many years — sometimes decades
  • The cumulative cost of care can reach into the millions of dollars
  • Alzheimer's and dementia cases typically require care for 8 to 20 years
  • A 10-year dementia episode at $15,000/month could cost $1,800,000
  • At a 50% tax bracket, funding that same care out-of-pocket would require earning $3,600,000

Why This Matters for Your Planning

Many people assume their LTC needs will be limited in duration. The real-world claims data tells a different story. The question is not if you will need long-term care, but when — and whether you'll be financially prepared.

We generally recommend LTC policies that provide lifetime benefits. These are the only policies that guarantee your LTC needs will be met, no matter how long care is required.

The Win-Win Policy Structure

The type of policy we recommend has two possible outcomes — both favorable:

  1. If LTC is needed: The insurer pays up to $24,000/month for the joint lifetime of both insureds — potentially millions of dollars in benefits.
  2. If LTC is never needed: The policy pays a death benefit upon the second insured's death that exceeds the premiums paid.

In either case, the policyholder wins. See more on why LTC insurance can be a winner →
