About Us

Meet the team behind Insurance Review Services

Your Trusted Insurance Professionals

When it comes to long term care insurance, we know that making the right choices for you and your family can be daunting. To help you make the right decision we take a commonsense approach – we ask questions, listen to your answers and only then recommend the type of LTC policy that will fit your particular needs.

Meet Withbert W. Payne, CPA

Withbert W. Payne is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and licensed insurance agent who has dedicated his career to helping individuals and families navigate the complexities of long-term care insurance. With a unique background that spans both accounting and insurance, Bert brings a comprehensive financial perspective to every client engagement.

Our Approach

Our firm is staffed by CPAs/licensed insurance agents who specialize in reviewing and auditing LTC policies. We believe that the best insurance decision is one made with full information and a clear understanding of your personal financial situation.

  • We listen first — every client situation is unique
  • We analyze your current coverage and financial position
  • We present options tailored to your specific needs and budget
  • We recommend policies that provide lifetime benefits when possible
  • All reviews are complimentary — insurance companies compensate us for our services

Why a CPA Makes the Difference

Most insurance agents focus solely on the insurance product. As CPAs, we also consider the tax implications, estate planning opportunities, and overall financial impact of your LTC coverage. This dual perspective allows us to recommend solutions that not only protect you from the high cost of long-term care but also serve your broader financial goals.

All of our reviews are complimentary. We can do this because the insurance companies compensate us for our services. You get expert, unbiased advice at no cost to you.
