Payne is a Chartered Accountant (CA), Certified Public Accountant (CPA), and CGMA with 58 years of experience in accounting and finance — and 25 years as a licensed, independent insurance broker. This rare combination of credentials means every client receives the rigorous financial analysis of a seasoned CPA alongside the market expertise of a dedicated insurance professional.

Bert has been a licensed insurance broker since 2001, specializing exclusively in long-term care insurance. He personally reviews every client's policy, explains his findings in plain language, and recommends solutions tailored to each client's specific financial situation — all at no cost. His CPA background means he always views LTC planning in the context of the complete financial picture: tax advantages, estate planning considerations, and cash flow impact.

As an independent broker, Bert represents no single carrier. He compares policies across the full market to find the best fit for each client's health profile, assets, and goals. His only obligation is to you.

Why Work With a CPA?

Most insurance agents are licensed to sell — but few have the financial training to audit. As a CPA and Chartered Accountant, Bert evaluates every policy through the lens of tax efficiency, cash flow impact, and long-term financial security. That's a level of analysis you won't find at a typical insurance agency. He doesn't just ask "what does this policy cover?" — he asks "is this the most financially efficient way to protect your assets?"
