Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need your services?

Insurance companies are constantly developing new insurance products and changing their pricing policies. The LTC insurance market has changed dramatically over the years, and policies that were the best choice five years ago may no longer be optimal today. Our CPA and licensed insurance expertise allows us to evaluate your current coverage objectively and recommend improvements if warranted.

Can my insurance agent provide this service?

We recommend seeking a review from someone independent of your original agent to avoid a conflict of interest and to obtain a different perspective. Your original agent has an incentive to maintain the status quo. As independent reviewers, we have no such conflict — our only goal is to find the best policy for your situation.

Is a LTC policy mandatory?

States like Washington have already implemented long-term care taxes on employees who do not have qualifying LTC insurance. California and other states plan to introduce a similar tax on employees who do not own a long-term care insurance policy. Premiums typically rise when states mandate coverage. Purchasing a policy now may exempt you from future taxes and lock in lower premiums.

What is single-premium LTC insurance?

Single-premium LTC insurance allows you to pay a one-time lump sum in exchange for lifetime long-term care coverage. For example, long-term care in a Skilled Nursing Facility today costs $12,000 a month or $144,000 a year. Average lifetime premiums are approximately $117,024 per person, with:

  • Guaranteed cash surrender value
  • A $400,000 death benefit if LTC services are never needed — a 61% gain for the estate
  • Lifetime coverage if LTC is needed

This type of policy protects couples from financial hardship while offering a guaranteed financial return if care is never required.

What is the cost of a review by a CPA?

All of our reviews are complimentary. We can do this because the insurance companies compensate us for our services. You receive expert, unbiased CPA-level analysis of your LTC insurance needs at absolutely no cost to you.
