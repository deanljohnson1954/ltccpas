NOW is The Time for Long Term Planning

NOW is the Time for Long Term Care Planning

Most people seeking coverage on their own have personal experiences with a loved one needing and paying for care. NOW is the time to start planning for your long term care needs.

The older you get, the more expensive LTC insurance becomes. As you age, you have a greater chance of becoming uninsurable and thus unable to obtain a LTC policy.

Statistically, "70% of people turning age 65 can expect to use some form of long-term care" during their lives.

Why Planning Ahead Matters

Why is planning ahead for long-term care so important? Consider the following benefits:

  • Not relying on children or family with caregiving responsibilities
  • Protecting your retirement assets
  • Ensuring that critical choices remain yours

Common Reasons People Delay — And Why They Shouldn't

There are several reasons many people don't plan ahead:

  • Maybe you think that you won't be dependent on others or need assistance even as you age.
  • Or maybe you think you have time to buy long-term care insurance.
  • Or you are a high net worth individual who plans to self-insure.

However, today's policies can pay for a lifetime of care for the cost of one year of a refundable premium if LTC is never needed — a win-win for the insured. High net worth individuals especially benefit from this approach, as it transfers risk efficiently while preserving estate value.

Whatever your reason for hesitating, give us a call and let's discuss your options.
