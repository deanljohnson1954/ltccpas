Key Findings

Six observations from the 2026 carrier matrix.

⚠️

Lifetime Benefits Are Rare

Only one of the seven carriers offers genuine lifetime (unlimited) LTC benefits. The remainder impose caps tied to the policy face amount or a defined benefit pool.

🏆

Hybrid Structures Lead on Flexibility

Hybrid life/LTC products consistently offer guaranteed premiums and a death benefit, unlike traditional standalone LTC. The trade-off is typically a higher initial premium.

🔓

Cash Indemnity Provides Flexibility

Carriers that pay benefits without receipts or pre-approval give flexibility for clients who self-direct or arrange informal care.

📈

Premium Stability Matters Over Time

Traditional LTC carriers without guaranteed premiums have historically passed rate increases to policyholders. Rate-increase history is an essential part of any rigorous evaluation.

💰

IRA Funding Is a Significant Development

The ability to fund a hybrid LTC structure using IRA or 401(k) assets meaningfully expands planning options for clients with qualified-plan accumulations.

📋

Traditional LTC Still Has a Place

Traditional carriers can offer high monthly benefit leverage and accept applicants to higher ages. For some profiles, these structures remain relevant.
