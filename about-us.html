Independent Analysis — Not Product Sales

Most financial assets are reviewed regularly. Insurance portfolios are often left untouched for years. As a California CPA, a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England & Wales (FCA), and a former Price Waterhouse auditor in London, I apply the same independent, audit-grade discipline to life and long-term care insurance that auditors apply to financial statements.

Rather than starting with a new insurance proposal, I begin with the policies you already own. That independent approach frequently identifies opportunities to strengthen guarantees, enhance living benefits, reduce unnecessary long-term costs, improve capital efficiency, and preserve family wealth.

Every recommendation begins with one question:

“Is your existing coverage already the best solution?”

Sometimes the answer is yes. Sometimes it is surprisingly not.
