E-News – January 1, 2023

HAPPY NEW YEAR — AND A NEW FOCUS ON LTC PLANNING

As we begin 2023, long-term care planning has never been more important. The combination of rising care costs, state-level mandates gaining momentum, and an aging population makes this the ideal time to review your LTC coverage.

THE LTC LANDSCAPE IN 2023

Washington State's landmark long-term care mandate has served as a wake-up call for many Americans. Several states — including California — are actively studying similar programs. The California Long Term Care Task Force continues its work under AB 567, with a mandate program on the horizon.

KEY FACTS FOR 2023

  • A private room in a skilled nursing facility now costs up to $15,000/month in major metro areas
  • Alzheimer's and dementia cases continue to rise, with care needs often lasting 8–20 years
  • 70% of people turning 65 will need some form of long-term care
  • The cost of self-insuring doubles in higher tax brackets
